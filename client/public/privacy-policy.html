Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Bank Teller 1988 is a single-player simulation game that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game operates entirely offline and does not require user accounts or personal data.

2. Game Data

All game progress, scores, and achievements are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not shared with any third parties.

3. Advertising

Our game may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information such as:

  • Device type and operating system
  • Advertising identifier (for personalized ads)
  • General location (country/region level only)

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information about Google's privacy practices, visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

Our game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal information to play.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information at risk. All game data remains on your local device.

6. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service integrated into our app is Google AdMob for advertising. We do not integrate any other third-party services that collect personal data.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the app and on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the app store where you downloaded the game.

9. Data Retention

Game data is stored locally on your device and is retained until you delete the app or clear its data. We do not store any data on our servers.

10. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You have full control over your local game data through your device settings.
